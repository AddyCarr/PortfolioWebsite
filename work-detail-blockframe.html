Blockframe

A lightweight NFT gallery & minting tool to easily share your NFTs

Utilising React, NextJS & CSS Modules - as part of a web3 bootcamp, myself and two team mates built this in 5 days as a way to showcase your NFTs in a quick and lightweight manner.

A fetch call to the Morlais API is used to pull in the NFTs and display them in a neat flex grid.
Simple minting functionality to come, inspired to allow easy access to minting for those who are not familiar with the process and without the need for an account on platforms such as OpenSea.
